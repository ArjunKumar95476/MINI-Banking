About Us

State Bank of India provides Account holders with a Mini statement service through which they can enquire about recent debit/credit transactions made in the account. There are different ways to access SBI mini-statement such as SBI Quick Banking, missed call banking, SMS banking, mobile & net banking. However, to access SBI mini statement, the customer must get his phone number registered with the bank. Let us find various ways to get SBI mini-statement SBI mini statement contains all your transaction details carried out through various modes such as NEFT, RTGS, IMPS, UPI, etc. You can get the State Bank of India mini statement both online and offline. If you are not familiar with the internet, you can generate a mini statement by visiting the ATM or the bank branch. If you are well-versed with the internet and have your mobile number registered with the bank, it can become easier for you to get the mini statement using several methods such as netbanking, SBI mobile banking, WhatsApp banking, etc.
